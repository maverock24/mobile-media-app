# Interesting Research Questions in the Area of AI (A)

1. **Alignment and Safety**: How can we ensure that highly capable AI systems robustly align with intended human values, even as they undergo recursive self-improvement or generalized learning?
2. **Interpretability**: What mathematical frameworks or visualization techniques can perfectly map the internal activations of large-scale neural networks to understandable human concepts?
3. **Efficiency and Architecture**: What novel neural architectures can completely eliminate the quadratic scaling bottlenecks of current Transformer models while maintaining or exceeding their reasoning capabilities?
4. **Data Scarcity**: How can AI systems achieve human-level few-shot learning capabilities without relying on massive, internet-scale datasets for pre-training?
5. **Multi-modality**: How can models be designed to inherently reason across entirely different sensory inputs (e.g., text, video, spatial movement, chemical sensors) without relying on text as an intermediary representation?
6. **Robotics and Embodiment**: What are the most effective methods for transferring complex reasoning capabilities from stationary generative models to autonomous, embodied agents operating in unpredictable physical environments?
7. **Bias and Fairness**: Can we develop automated, provable mechanisms that guarantee a machine learning model will not exhibit bias against protected attributes across any subset of its training distribution?
